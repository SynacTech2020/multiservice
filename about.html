About Us

Story about our shop

We Offer a full range of Professional equipment to our clients

AMS began its journey in the year 2015 from Aurangabad. This company was started with sound experience in medical engineering field. Combining with experience, technical expertise, dedicated service and sound business strategies, we have steadily grown over the years and have demonstrated the potential to become a major provider in Hospital Medical Gas Pipeline System, Medical equipments and related products. Our offered services find wide application in hospitals and other medical institutions. All our offered gas pipelines are trusted for their well-planned medical gas distribution systems. Offered range of our products provides medical life support network in operation theatres, Intensive Care Unit’s and Critical Care Unit’s. We execute maintenance and repairing services under the supervisory guidance of project managers and our experienced team of engineers, technicians and other allied workers. Customers' preferences and specifications are emphasized at our end.

Meet Our Team We have a qualified and experienced team which is dedicated and skilled at its jobs. Their industrious efforts lead to provide unbeatable products and services thereby offering utmost level of satisfaction to the clients.
